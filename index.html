About

Evilpanda is a collaborative design & web development studio dedicated to merging all forms of art into design. We work with traditional artists, ui/ux designers, developers, filmmakers, musicians, animators and many more to blend art with great functional web and print presence.

Our home base in Los Angeles, CA, Evilpanda has an eclectic blend of collaborators and an endless amount of inspiration.
